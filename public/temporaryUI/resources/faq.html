What is Noumi?

What is Noumi?
Noumi is a self-refining Digital Twin — an AI that remembers your context, learns your working preferences, and executes tasks on your behalf. Unlike general AI assistants that reset with every conversation, Noumi compounds with use. The longer you work with it, the less you have to explain.
How is Noumi different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
Most AI tools treat every conversation as a blank slate. Noumi persists your context, rules, and preferences across sessions. Over time, it stops needing instructions — it already knows how you work.
Who is Noumi built for?
Noumi is built for professionals who handle complex, recurring work — product managers, journalists, analysts, solutions engineers, and anyone who's tired of starting over with every AI conversation.

Features

What does "Persistent Memory" mean?
When you share context with Noumi — your project background, writing style, recurring tasks — it remembers. Next session, next week, next month: you don't explain it again.
How do "Self-Evolving Skills" work?
Every time you correct Noumi's output or set a preference, it stores that as a rule. Future tasks apply your rules automatically — no retraining, no re-explaining. Your corrections become its defaults.
What is "Agentic Execution"?
Noumi can break down complex tasks, use tools, and complete multi-step work on its own. You describe the goal. Noumi handles the steps, and comes back with finished work.
Can Noumi handle files and documents?
Yes. You can upload documents, transcripts, research notes, and other materials into your workspace. Noumi works with them as part of your persistent context.
Can I use Noumi for my team?
Noumi is built for individual professionals today. Team features are on our roadmap.

Privacy & Data

Is my data private?
Your workspace, memory, and uploaded files are private to you. We do not use your data to train shared models. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored securely in the cloud. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can cancel your account at any time, and your data will be deleted within 30 days. You can also request deletion of specific content. Contact us at official@noumi.ai.

Getting Started

How do I get started?
Sign up for free, set up your workspace with your context and working preferences, and give Noumi its first task. Most users find the value within their first few sessions.
Do I need technical skills to use Noumi?
No. Noumi is designed for professionals, not developers. If you can write a message, you can use Noumi.
Is there a free plan?
Yes. Noumi offers a free tier so you can get started without a credit card. See our Pricing page for details on what's included.
